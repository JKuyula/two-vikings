About Us

We've been making delicious food for more than 30 years

Welcome to our charming fish and chips restaurant nestled in the heart of Amanzimtoti CBD! Our commitment to quality shines through in every aspect, from the freshest catch of the day to the carefully hand-cut chips. Indulge your senses in our wide selection of delectable seafood dishes, each crafted with passion and expertise. Whether you prefer classic fish and chips, succulent calamari, or mouthwatering prawns, our menu offers something to satisfy every craving. With friendly and attentive staff, a vibrant ambiance, and a dedication to culinary excellence, our fish and chips restaurant in Amanzimtoti is the perfect destination for seafood lovers seeking an unforgettable taste adventure. Come join us, and let us delight you with the very best of the ocean's bounty!
